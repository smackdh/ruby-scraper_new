        <div class="entry-content">
			<p class="container-content text-center mb-1">Looking for a brewery? Brewers Association members are denoted by the BA logo and <a href="https://www.brewersassociation.org/statistics-and-data/craft-brewer-definition/">Independent craft breweries</a> are denoted by the <a href="https://www.brewersassociation.org/programs/independent-craft-brewer-seal/">Independent Craft Brewer Seal</a>.
